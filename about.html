Our Mission

Driven by Curiosity,
Guided by Science

Founded in 2000, our institute has been at the forefront of deep-sky observation and planetary science. We operate six world-class telescopes across three continents, giving us an unbroken view of the sky around the clock.

Our mission is simple: push the boundaries of human knowledge by studying everything from exoplanet atmospheres to var(--color-deep-space) hole dynamics — and share those discoveries with the world.
