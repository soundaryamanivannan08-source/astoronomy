Frequently Asked Questions

Can the general public visit the main observatory?

Yes, we host public observation nights every Friday and Saturday evening. Registration via our event board is highly encouraged ahead of schedule.

How can I submit data collaborations for research?

Please utilize the standard contact form selecting 'Research Partnerships' or forward documentation assets directly over to our engineering department group email.

Are there membership programs available?

Absolutely. We feature annual dynamic plans covering individual stargazers up to academic institutional profiles offering dedicated portal remote system links.
