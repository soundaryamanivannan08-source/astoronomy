Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Do I need any prior astronomy knowledge to join?
Not at all. Our Explorer plan and beginner sessions are specifically designed for people with zero prior experience. Our astronomers explain everything in plain language from the very start.
What happens if it is cloudy on my session night?
We monitor weather conditions 72 hours in advance. If observing is not possible, we automatically reschedule your session to the next clear night at no extra charge. You will receive an email and SMS notification.
Can I bring my own telescope or camera equipment?
Absolutely. We welcome your gear and our astronomers are happy to help set it up, collimate it, and get the best out of it during the session. If you do not own equipment, we provide everything.
